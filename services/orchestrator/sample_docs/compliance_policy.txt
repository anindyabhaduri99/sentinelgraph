Internal Compliance Policy — AI-Assisted Recommendations (Fictional)

Any AI-generated financial recommendation exceeding $5,000 in notional
value must be routed to human-in-the-loop review before being presented
to a client.

All AI-assisted responses must include a disclaimer identifying the
content as AI-generated and subject to human oversight.

Confidence scores below 0.9 on the internal evaluator model require
mandatory escalation; the system may not auto-respond in these cases.

All requests, agent decisions, tool invocations, and responses must be
logged to the immutable audit store for a minimum retention period of
7 years, per recordkeeping requirements.
