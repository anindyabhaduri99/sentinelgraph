Client Servicing FAQ (Fictional)

Q: How do I request a portfolio rebalancing review?
A: Submit a request through the client portal under "Portfolio Actions".
Reviews are typically completed within 3 business days.

Q: What is the process for disputing a trade execution?
A: Contact client servicing within 48 hours of trade confirmation.
Disputes are escalated to the trade operations team for review.

Q: How are budget or allocation recommendations generated?
A: Recommendations are AI-assisted, based on historical allocation
performance, and reviewed by a human advisor before being finalized.
